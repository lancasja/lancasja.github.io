Jonathan Lancaster

San Francisco based artist, Jonathan Lancaster, has been honing his coding and design skills for the past several years. Originally a classically trained saxophonist, Jonathan is combining his skills in technology and design with his many years of experience as a musician to bring out his perfect storm of artistic expression.

With a deep passion for performance and songwriting, Jonathan aims to integrate technology and great design into his works to create epic human experiences.
